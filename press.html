Crowdfunding Campaign
When I got into college, a few friends of mine and I realized that we couldn't pay for the total cost of going to college abroad. We then started a crowdfunding campaign so we could attend the best universities in the globe and advance science. Our campaign went viral, and my page alone got over 30k Facebook likes and almost R$ 60k (equivalent to ~USD 30k at the time).
